Warranty Info

A 1 year warranty is given to all devices, excluding braces and hot/cold therapy units which carry a 6-month warranty on the unit and pad. This warranty is for replacement of like items only. If we no longer stock them, then we will upgrade you to a like model. Outdated technology is not covered by warranty if the item is no longer available on the common market as a new product. Warranty policy does not include accessories supplied with the device. For example, lead wires, battery, and carrying case.

Shipping is excluded from warranty coverage and may be assessed on a replacement.
